About Me

In 2000 I designed and built my first website with NetObjects Fusion, and that is where my passion for web developemnt started. I thought it was just a fun learning experience, but not long after completing my project the company I was managing at the time had to revamp its website. I was able to manage the entire project, based on what I had learned by building my own website.

Later, while working for AAA Carolinas, I again used my web and database design experience and implemented various IT technology platforms facilitating report/service automation such as: SQL database on UNIX OS and automated ERS service deployment utilizing Automated Vehicle Locating (AVL) technology. The SQL database was a 12-month project that I managed from conception to final implementation, fully automating the Emergency Road Service reporting. This transformed how AAA Carolinas monitored and controlled service deployment to its members, and saving AAA Carolinas 3+ million dollars within the first year of implementation.

I again I found myself utilizing my web development skills while working for a marketing company. I sold, designed and managed the first B2B website project produced by the company. That's when I realized that I was meant to be a web developer.

I am proud to say that I am a life-long learner with a passion for web development, formally trained in the UNC Charlotte Web Development program as a Full Stack Developer. I am very active in the coding community, which you can view under my Meetup Groups, where I participate in training events as well as civic Hackathons.
